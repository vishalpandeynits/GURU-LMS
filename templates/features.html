Features for Admins

  1. Admins can create classrooms with unique entry protocols that they can then distribute amongst their students and teachers who want to join classroom.
  2. Admins can inspect in any classroom at any time and see the progress of teachers and students.
  3. Admins can manage the role of admins, teachers and student without breaking any sweat.
  4. Admins would also have the permissions of teacher, which allow them to send resources, assignments and announcements at any time.
  5. Admins can authorize students to post in the classroom.
  6. Admins can contact anyone directly from within the web app
  7. No one can join any classroom without admin's permissions unless admin have already given permission to join.

Features for Teachers

  1. Teachers can provide assignments and set deadlines.
  2. Teachers can view students who had submitted on time, who couldn’t, who did and who didn’t. Teachers can also be able to assign marks and generate a report of marks assigned to each student for any assignment in MS-Excel.
  3. Teachers can post resources in their classroom and also in the resource hub.
  4. Teachers can authorize students to post in the classroom.
  5. Teachers can contact students directly from within the web app
  6. Teachers can add as many subjects as they want in the classroom.
  7. Teachers can create polls and specify who can participate in them. Polls result will be shown accordingly.

Features for Students

  1. Students can join in any classroom with the unique classroom code provided.
  2. Live update of activities will be provide to students via notification system and E-mail. So that they wouldn't miss anything.
  3. Students can submit their assignments and classwork without breaking a sweat and get marks.
  4. Students can contact their teacher directly from within the web app.
  5. Students can post public comments on the resources, assignments and announcement assigned and clear their doubts.
  6. Switching between classrooms and subjects is seamless.
Note: The person who will create a classroom will be treated as an admin. After that other people can join this classroom. Then Admin can then create subjects and assign it to a teacher. Only admin and teacher are allowed to post contents in this subject, unless they give permission to any other student to upload resources(This user will not be allowed to add assignments or announcements). Also admins can make admin to other members or remove from the position of admins. Admins will also have permission to remove members from classroom. There are lot of other features besides it. Happy exploring to you.
